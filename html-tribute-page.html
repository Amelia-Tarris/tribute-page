Paul Walker: A Tribute Page
Tribute Video

Paul Walker is most famous for his role in the Fast and Furious franchise as Brian O'Conner, but he had been beloved by the public since his first appearance on screen when he was just 2 years old.

While he was best known for "Fast and Furious," he appeared in other films such as "Into the Blue" (2005) and "Joy Ride" (2001).

Paul Walker tragically died in a car accident on November 30, 2013, in Santa Clarita, California. The accident occurred while attending a charity event for ROWW. His untimely death had a significant impact on his fans and the film industry.

After the tragic accident, Paul left behind four siblings and a daughter.

Paul was close friends with fellow co-stars Vin Diesel and Tyrese Gibson

Facts about Paul:

  1. Passionate about marine biology
  2. He was a racecar driver in real life
  3. Vin Diesel is the Godfather to his daughter
  4. He performed all his own stunts in the first Fast & Furious film

Paul Walker's sudden and tragic passing was a significant loss to the entertainment industry, and he is fondly remembered by fans and colleagues alike.
